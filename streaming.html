Streaming laptop

Our premium laptop aimed at providing the greatest Streaming experience to users in 2021. The goal of the user research is to understand the consumer needs and the interaction between the Environment and Streaming devices. To bring out the persona and user journey in order to drive the product core team to make the right decision on product development and deliver the hero experience to the end user.

I am the user researcher and curator. Therefore, I should take care of this project from end-to-end : Start from defining the scope of research, arrange the research structure, conduct user interviews, to the very end to validate experience deliverables. I am also the pioneer on introducing design thinking methodology into the core team.

Approach

Step 1. Business strategy

The product targeted two different markets : North America and Great China. First step is to understand the current market key holders on streaming. North America is taken by Netflix with a browser. But for the China market, there is more flexibility. We chose the iQiyi application user on the laptop as our main target user.





Step 2. User Research

Our recruiting goal is to find 6 participants with the streaming behavior on each market. And then we conducted user interviews to understand their needs and current pain points. Then doing the affinity diagram in order to transform the findings into user insights and motivation.




Step 3. Design Thinking Workshop

Invite the stakeholders to join the brainstorming workshop. It's important for the team to align the goal and allocate the responsibility. In the workshop, we also invite users into the discussion, so that we can always keep user experience as the center.




Step 4. Experience Curation

After bringing out the hero experience that we would like to deliver, the curator should always keep an eye on the developing progress and set up the successful criteria on experience. Then set a checkpoint to make sure the product is ready for user testing.





Step 5. Usability Testing

The COVID-19 forced our testing to be virtual. We sent the devices to users then hosted the online testing session for 10 ppl. The testing goal is to validate the experience is well delivered to the end user and the user story will really happen in the future. The findings also become an improvement perspective in our next generation product.
